Traffic Jam Help

    Overview
    Object of Traffic Jam
    To move the red van (sometimes 2 red vans) out of the traffic jam using the exit at the right.
    Vans can only move forward and backwards in the direction they are facing. They cannot turn.
    They must stop when blocked by the kerb or another van.

    Instructions
    Move items by dragging with the stylus.
    Good luck and why not try all 40 puzzles?

    Changing Player

    To add your name to the players list
    • tap Switch Id
    • tap one of the 5 player lines
    • tap change name
    • enter your name
    • tap one of:
        Keep if you are correcting your name
        Set to 1 if you are adding your name for the first time
        Cancel if you don't want to change the name
    • tap OK or Cancel
