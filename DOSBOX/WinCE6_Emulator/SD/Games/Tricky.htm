Tricky Disk Help

    Overview
    The game starts at the easy level with a shuffled Disk. Move the casing and twist the spokes to align each coloured quarter-spoke with the same colour on the casing, as shown below.

    Stylus Moves
    Hints

     

    Stylus Moves
    • To shuffle at any time tap New -> Shuffle.
    • To switch levels tap Level and choose a level.
    • To rotate the disk, touch the background and drag the stylus
    • To twist the top or bottom half touch the casing and drag the stylus
    • To twist a spoke touch the spoke and drag the stylus
