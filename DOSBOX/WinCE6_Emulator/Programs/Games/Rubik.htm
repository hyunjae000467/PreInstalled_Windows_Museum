Rubik Help

    Overview
    The game starts with a shuffled Rubik Cube. Tap and drag on the cube to make a move. The cube will snap into position when you finish dragging. Tap and drag on the background to move the cube into a convenient position or to look at the rear.

    Stylus Moves
    Hints

     

    Stylus Moves

    • To shuffle at any time tap Tools -> Shuffle.
    • To twist the cube drag a facet in the direction you want it to move.
    • To rotate the whole cube tap the background and drag in the direction you want it to move.
